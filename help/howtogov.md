# Best Practices for newly minted Government Members:

## Everyone
You should be able to write in #fed-gov in Discord. It's a private channel. PM and MPs can write in #the-house. This is used for law-talk, so people know what's going to hit them and why. Don't make secret laws.

## Member of Parliament:

1. Your job is to make sure taxation and budget works. Without that, rest of the government can't do anything.
2. QoL laws
- Hunters being able to hunt on claimed property
- Motivating Loggers to not leave stumps
3. Recurring Issues:
These are laws where imports are possible, they might need minor adjustments to fit each cycle.
- CO2 Emissions.
Unproblematic until Oil Drilling releases. Have a law to keep them in check ready, or temperature rise will make your Farmers unhappy and water rise will make you sleep with the fishes eventually.
Both putting a cost on pollution or a more heavy handed Emission Permit System have worked well in the past.
- Wealth Tax
For when you notice that all money pools on 1-3 individuals and your other taxation scheme hasn't fixed that yet
- Property Tax
Helps in motivating people to not have insanely large claims
- Electricity Law
Puts a cost on power consumption, pays for producing it. Ideally don't pay for Combustion Generators, you'll want to disincentivize people running them.
- Farming Districts
Your Farmers will, especially at the start of the cycle, have issues protecting their crops. Doing this either via claim paper rebates or with districts has worked out well in the past.
- Titles
Some titles, like Ocean Mining Licence or Explosive Licence are for you to worry about. You can give them to *almost* everyone. Why almost? Grief-Prevention for Explosives, mostly. Either do a manual discord thread, where people can request the title (Payment is an Option). Or give it out based on a criteria. For example having spent more than 10hrs on White Tiger.

## Prime Minister
1. Ministers. Your job is to make sure you have Ministers, so you don't need to handle their job yourself. If you don't have any ready, use #newspaper on Discord to ask for them. Education > Infrastructure > Culture is roughly the ranking of importance, describing how quickly your constituents will write you angry DMs if missing.
2. Claim Papers. Sell them, usually for like +-100 Euro per Paper and 400+ Euro per Claim Stake You're the only one who can. Use the store in your PM office. Don't sell them all at once, people will want some in later weeks as well. Bank Accounts can be Federal Treasury or the PM Bank Account.
3. PM Bank Account?! Your MPs allot you a budget for projects, like a bigger more beautiful office. Or a ballroom.
4. Meteor Store. WT uses special Laser Recipes, not the base game ones. They're more complex. A lot. Either handle it yourself or hire a Federal Secretary to take care of it. Ingame F1 -> Documentation -> Victory Conditions has more details.
5. Unclaim Requests. Sometimes people will come to you with an Unclaim Request signed off by a judge. Not for the first 3 days though. If they do, you need an Executive Desk process it there.
6. Trust in your MPs and other Gov Members, even GMs. Chances are, they've done what you do and are very willing to help you.

## Minister of Education
1. Congrats, you have a pre-setup University. Your job is to make sure the research-schedule posted in #law-gazette works out. Your tool for that will be Research Tables, Laboratories (Not Week 1) and a Store.
2. On your first day, that'll usually be Masonry and Carpentry asap. Then already prepare for the next day's research. It's good practice to buy research for future unlocks 1-2 days early.
3. When? "/time now" ingame will tell you which day it is. As soon as the Server Day in the schedule is hit, you can start crafting and selling Research.
4. Scrolls. Do not right click books. We know it works on other servers. But here we craft scrolls. Best not release skills until there are 10 Scrolls ready for sale.
5. Buy Prices?! Try to not overpay too much or underpay too much. You have wiggle room, but if you're unsure, ask in chat. People want your money and they're willing to give you pricing opinions.
6. Sell Prices?! Scrolls don't necessarily need to perfectly reflect the price it cost you to craft everything. You can subsidize scrolls. Especially if you feel that'll lock a large part of the market out of taking especially late game skills

## Minister of Infrastructure
1. Congrats, you have a pre-setup Federal Highway. Your job will be to make sure it first gets Stone Roads, then Asphalt Concrete and Transmission Poles later. Remember, you're a manager. Make sure you motivate others.
2. Road Law. Congress can import one for you, which you should absolutely make them do. It has multiple accounts, which control pricing. Price per Stone Road for example. Or per Asphalt Concrete. Or per Bridge Material etc. Look into the law, it has explanations.
3. Ideally, you post a layout on how you want roads with Asphalt to look like. It helps your road workers to have a common goal to strive for. 
4. Road Workers? You can either give the title to Everyone or make a Discord Post and give it out manually. We've tried both and they both work.
5. Later weeks, you also take care of paying for docks. Sometimes people come to you for that, sometimes a #newspaper post. Tunnel Requests, Tunnel Lining, Waterway disputes or generally anything that sounds like it could be used by a vehicle is your problem.
6. Transmission Poles for electric power along the highway are also your problem. Make sure there's a decent global grid.
7. Electric Power? Yeah... you can. Often towns or countries ask for assistance here. Like money for Industrial Generators (IGs) or Green Power. IGs most often need your monetary support, because they're really expensive. Governments will likely come to you.

## Minister of Culture
1. You're here for Fun, Events and just generally making sure not just hard, productive truths are met. You have the most freedom in what to do with your budget. Don't become corrupt.
Options:
- Someone asks you for money for a beautiful building, that's not just housing min-max? Sounds good!
- MoI declined to fund a beautiful dock, because he just wants a functional one? You can do that!
- Federal Dinner Party? Why not! Find a cook, pay him decently (not handsomely!) and announce a time in #newspaper or #notice-board
- Want to build something yourself or contract it out? Yup, can do!
- Want to turn the Federal Presidents Palace into a beautiful art museum, showcasing the importance of proper Waste Management? You'll make the president angry, but it's an option!
